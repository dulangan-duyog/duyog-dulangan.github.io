DULANGAN UNIFIED YOUTH GROUP

The Dulangan Unified Youth Group is a IP youth organization under the Dulangan Unified Ancestral Domain

Learn More

Mission

Recognition of identify, development and training of IP youths towards engangament in sustainable living and conservation of Lumad culture.

Vision

Unity to strengthen conservation and protection of practices in the Ancestral Domain.

Goal

Dulangan Unified Youth Group overall, long-term goal is the protection and sustainable development of Higaonon's ancestral domain and it's culture through the ip youths.

Our Story

The Dulangan Unified Youth Group started in 2017, it servers as the ip youth backbone of the Dulangan Unified Ancestral Domain for empowerment and training of youths that will eventually be the next leader in the community.

DULANGAN UNIFIED YOUTH ORGANIZATION

SUB-ORGANIZATIONS

Projects

The Dulangan Unified Youth Group will engage in various socio-cultural, economic and environmental conservation .

Ecological Conservation

DUYOG aims to contribute to the conservation of ecological systems within the ancestral domain through various projects and programs including water source rehabilitation, reforestation, wildlings collection and ordinance for protection of ancestral lands

IP Education

DUYOG aims to educate ip youths on the culture and tradition of the Higaonon through IP school projects

Youth Organization

DUYOG aims to established youth organizations within the ancestral to better mold ip youths for the next generation.

Livelihood

DUYOG aims to established Livelihood projects especially in agriculture sector such as diversified farming system/multi-cropping, traditional farming and organic farming methods.

Dulangan Unified Ancestral Domain

The Dulangan Unified Ancestral Domain the mother organization of DUYOG is located in the Municipality of Opol, Province of Misamis Oriental. Composed of 8 barangays, 6 from the Municipality of Opol and 2 from the Municipality of Manticao with a total land area of 18,000 hectares
